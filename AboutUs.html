ABOUT US

Meet our CEO

At the heart of Ernails PH is our dynamic and visionary CEO, Pauline Mapoy. At just 19 years old, Pauline is a first-year student at FEU Institute of Technology, pursuing a degree in Information Technology. Her passion for beauty and technology has driven her to create Ernails PH, a brand that seamlessly blends innovation with style.

Pauline's journey is a testament to her entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to excellence. Despite her young age, she has successfully led Ernails PH to become a respected name in the nail care industry. Her unique perspective, combining technical expertise and a keen eye for design, has been instrumental in developing products that meet the highest standards of quality and creativity.

Our Mission

At Ernails PH, our mission is to empower and inspire individuals through high-quality, innovative nail products that celebrate creativity and self-expression. We strive to bring beauty and confidence to our customers by providing a diverse range of press-on nails and nail polish that cater to every style and occasion. Committed to excellence, we focus on sustainability, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement to ensure that everyone can enjoy beautiful, salon-quality nails in the comfort of their own home. Our dedication to inclusivity and community drives us to create products that are not only accessible but also make a positive impact on the lives of our customers.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for beauty and creativity, Ernails PH began as a vision to revolutionize the nail care industry. What started as a small idea among friends quickly blossomed into a thriving brand dedicated to providing top-quality press-on nails and nail polish products. Our journey is rooted in innovation and a commitment to delivering products that empower individuals to express their unique style effortlessly.

From humble beginnings, Ernails PH has grown to become a trusted name known for its dedication to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Each product is crafted with care, ensuring that every nail enthusiast can achieve salon-quality results at home. As we continue to expand our product line and reach, our mission remains unchanged: to inspire confidence and celebrate individuality through the art of nail care.
